Overview

About the Package:

When booking, you will need to communicate the number of passengers and the flight code. With your flight code our driver can check the flight status and arrive at the airport 10 minutes before your arrival. The driver will be waiting for you at the Meeting Point of your arrival terminal with a sign with your name on it, so that you can recognize him more easily.

Each transfer from Ciampino airport to Rome is carried out with luxury and black cars. The type of car changes according to the number of passengers to be transported.

For transfers of up to 3 people we use Mercedes Benz E sedans, instead for transfers of up to 8 passengers we use Mercedes Benz V minivans. Each car used is of the latest generation and equipped with every comfort to ensure comfortable and quality transfers.

Ciampino Airport to Rome City: transfer rates:

The rates for this transfer from Ciampino airport to Rome vary according to the number of passengers on board.

Rates by passengers N°:

  • 1 to 3 passengers: €60,00 (Sedan)
  • 4 to 6 passengers: €70,00 (Minivan)
  • 7 to 8 passengers: €80,00 (Minivan)

The above rates are inclusive of 10% VAT

Extra Costs:

Night transfers (from 10PM to 6AM) have a supplement of €10 on the total price. Transfers made on New Year’s Day have an extra cost of €20 on the total price.

Frequently asked questions

  • How much is a taxi from Ciampino airport to Rome Centre?
    A private taxi from Ciampino airport to Rome Center can cost from 60 to 80 euros depending on the number of passengers. Transfers up to 3 people are carried out with Mercedes Benz E sedans, while transfers up to 8 people are carried out with Mercedes Benz V minivans.
  • How do I get from Ciampino airport to Rome city Centre?
    To go from Ciampino airport to Rome center you can ask for a private transfer in a luxury car.
  • Does Ciampino airport have a train station?
    Yes, however it is not easily reachable on foot as you would have to go through a fast road not suitable for pedestrians.
  • How long does it take to get from Ciampino Airport to city centre?
    With a taxi or private transfer it will take approximately 30 minutes, or 1 hour by bus.
  • How long does it take to get from Rome Ciampino Airport (CIA) to Rome?
    By bus it will take about 60 minutes, while with a taxi or private transfer it will take half the time, or 30 minutes.
  • What is the cheapest way to get from Rome Ciampino Airport (CIA) to Rome?
    The cheapest way to get from Ciampino airport to Rome is via one of the buses at the airport, however your journey will be much longer. With a private transfer you will spend a little more but you will save time and will arrive directly at your location in Rome.
